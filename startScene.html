Stair Game

Survive and get down, touch as many platforms as you can!

Game Rule:

1. Penguin falls until touches the platform

2. Control the penguin to move left/right using the L/R arrow keys

3. The score increases with respect to the number of platforms that

    the penguin touches

4. When the penguin touches the thorn on the top/platform, life will be deducted by 4 units

5. Life can be gained by 2 units when penguin touches a platform

6. The time limit is 3 minutes

7. Game over when time's up, penguin cannot find a platform to stand or has no more life
